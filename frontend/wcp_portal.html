Terms, Conditions & Policy Scope — Part B & C (Bajaj Finserv Health OPD Policy)
Part B — Scope of Coverage & Terms
B.1 Benefits Covered

B.1.1 Prescribed Diagnostics

Covered: Outpatient diagnostic tests — biochemistry, haematology, immunology, microbiology, serology, pathology, X-ray, ultrasound, TMT — prescribed by a Registered Medical Practitioner. Availed at BFHL Network or Non-network providers, subject to plan limits.

Not Covered: AYUSH procedures; preventive/non-prescribed tests; tests not necessitated by illness; all invasive procedures (see Annexure C.4).

B.1.2 Tele / Insta Consultation

Covered: Audio/video/chat consultations via BFHL digital platform with Medical Practitioner/Physician/Doctor including AYUSH where available.

Not Covered: Consultations outside designated platform; specialties not supported (psychologist, dietician, cosmetic surgeon, audiologist, physiotherapist, speech therapist, embryologist); psychiatric/mental health counselling under this benefit.

B.1.3 In-clinic / Onsite Consultation

Covered: Consultations or second opinions from a Registered Medical Practitioner (including AYUSH) for Illness or Injury. Network or non-network up to plan limits.

Reimbursement Caps: GP — ₹600 | Specialist — ₹1,000 | Super Specialist — ₹1,500 per consultation.

B.1.4 Prescribed Pharmacy

Covered: Prescribed medicines (tablets, capsules, topical, liquids, suppositories, drops, inhalers, patches) within prescribed quantity. Prescription validity 30 days; 90 days for chronic diseases.

Not Covered: OTC/non-prescribed medicines; health supplements; nutraceuticals; vaccinations; medical devices; AYUSH medicines.

B.1.5 Dental Care

Covered: Acute treatment to natural teeth by licensed dentist. Covered: consultation, dental X-rays/OPG, scaling & polishing, fillings (silver/composite/GI), RCT (adults/children), pulpotomy, extractions (normal/surgical), recementation, fluoride treatment, oral surgery, metal crowns (clinically indicated).

Not Covered: Cosmetic/aesthetic procedures; bleaching; dentures; braces/aligners; retainers; space maintainers; night guards; FPD abutment RCT.

B.1.6 Vision Care

Covered: Medically necessary treatment by ophthalmologist/optometrist for eye problems. Spectacles, lenses & contact lenses covered up to ₹3,000 per insured life.

Not Covered: Frames; sunglasses (prescription or otherwise); day care procedures (cataract, laser correction, biometry).

B.1.7 Vaccination Cover

Covered: All WHO-approved vaccines with valid prescription/certificate, administered as per WHO schedule based on age and medical necessity.

Not Covered: Vaccines not recognised or approved by WHO.

B.1.8 Preventive Health Check-up (AHC)

Covered: Cashless health check-up at prescribed network hospitals/diagnostic centres available on BFHL app. Reimbursement for predefined comprehensive packages from registered labs/hospitals only.

Not Covered: Standalone tests or individually charged tests outside predefined packages.

B.1.9 Wellness Cover

Covered: Wellness benefits on BFHL app — Diet & Nutrition, Mental Wellness, Fitness/Gym access, Health Risk Assessment, Online Fitness Sessions, Step/Activity Tracker, Quit Smoking, Weight Management — up to policy limits. Deducted from Wellness Wallet.

Not Covered: Wellness benefits availed from any third-party platform outside BFHL app.

B.1.10 OPD Procedures

Covered: Reimbursement for in-clinic OPD procedures resulting from injuries and illnesses as per the Company's notified exhaustive list of procedures.

Not Covered: Any procedure not mentioned in the coverage list.

B.2 General Medical Exclusions
Weight/height management or obesity treatment (including morbid obesity) • Cosmetic, aesthetic and re-shaping treatments unless for reconstruction after Accident/cancer/burns • Experimental/investigational/unproven treatments • Congenital illness (internal and external) • Alopecia, wigs or toupees • Medical supplies (elastic stockings, diabetic test strips) • Venereal/sexually transmitted diseases; HIV/AIDS • Genetic studies • Physiotherapy sessions • Pregnancy/miscarriage complications (except due to Accident/Illness) • Day care procedures and maternity unless specifically covered • Sterility, infertility and assisted reproduction (IVF, ICSI) • Health check-ups/certificates for employment or travel • Expenses not medically necessary or beyond Reasonable & Customary charges • Naturopathy, acupressure, acupuncture, magnetic therapies; Patanjali/herbal therapies • Circumcision unless medically indicated • Menopause/General debility • Nervous breakdown/instability/self-injury • Alcohol/drug abuse including detoxification • Cross-pathy treatment • Pre/Post Hospitalisation OPD expenses (covered under IPD policy if any).
B.3 Utilisation Terms
  1. All claims must be raised on the Bajaj Finserv Health app only.
  2. Employee mobile number / Corporate email ID must be provided for app sign-up.
  3. All claims must be submitted within 30 days from the date of incidence.
  4. Prescriptions older than 30 days will not be considered; for chronic diseases, validity is 90 days.
  5. Cashless benefits availed from BFHL platform will not be considered for reimbursement under any benefit.
  6. Claims with uncleared deficiencies beyond 30 days from deficiency date will not be considered.
  7. Valid prescriptions, invoices, bills and reports must be provided as applicable.
  8. Benefits are not transferable unless the member is covered under the Base Policy.
  9. Unavailed consultations in the Policy Year cannot be carried forward to subsequent years.
  10. Minimum reimbursement claim amount must be greater than ₹200; lower bills must be clubbed.
  11. Duplicate claims for the same bill will be rejected including by spouses/common insured members.
  12. The Company reserves the right to reject claims if the provider is blacklisted on the platform.
  13. Inflated bills/medicines will be curtailed to Reasonable and Customary charges.
Part C — General Guidelines

C.1 Acceptable Age Band

E: 18–65 years  |  ES: 18–65 years

ESC: Employee/Spouse 18–65; Children 0–25 years

ESCP: Employee/Spouse/Children as above; Parents/Parents-in-law 0–85 years

C.2 Endorsement Terms

Additions: Midterm additions allowed for natural additions only, intimated within 45 days. Premium charged in full — no pro-rating. Effective from date of addition, not policy inception.

Deletions: Pro-rata refund for entire family on deletion, subject to nil claims. Intimation must be immediate on finalisation of last working day.

C.3 Cancellation / Termination

15 days written notice required. Refund scale (subject to nil claims):

Up to 1 month: 75%  |  Up to 3 months: 50%  |  Up to 6 months: 25%  |  Beyond 6 months: 0%

C.4 Fraud & Misrepresentation

Fraudulent claims or false statements will result in forfeiture of all benefits and premium paid. Amounts already paid must be repaid. Policy may be cancelled. This includes misrepresentation, concealment or any act fitted to deceive.

Annexure — Supporting Details

C.1 Chronic Disease Coverage (if applicable)

Diabetes mellitus/insipidus, Hypertension, COPD, Arthritis/Osteoarthritis/Rheumatoid arthritis, Hyper/Hypothyroidism, Bronchitis, Epilepsy, Migraine, Heart disease/Angina, Hypercholesterolemia, Asthma, Anaemia.

C.2 Claim Documentation Required

Consultation: Prescription + invoice/receipt  |  Diagnostics: Reports + invoice + doctor's advice  |  Pharmacy: Invoice + prescription  |  Dental: Dental prescription + X-rays + invoice  |  Vaccination: Certificate + invoice  |  Vision: Ophthalmologist prescription + invoice

C.3 Document Standards

Prescriptions must carry doctor's registration number and line of treatment. Chemist bills must include GSTIN and Drug Licence Number. Lab reports must be signed by a Pathologist. Diagnostic bills must carry GSTIN and doctor's referral. Blank documents with only signature/stamp are inadmissible.

C.4 Invasive Tests (Excluded from Diagnostics)

USG/CT/MRI guided biopsies; endoscopic biopsies; upper GI endoscopy; colonoscopy with biopsy; diagnostic laparoscopy; hysteroscopy; cystoscopy with biopsy; bronchoscopy with biopsy; CT angiography; coronary angiography; lumbar puncture; liquid biopsy; pleural procedures; lymph node biopsy and similar invasive procedures.

C.5 Acceptable Doctor Qualifications

GP: MBBS  |  AYUSH: BHMS/BAMS/BUMS/BSMS/BNYS  |  Medicine: MD/DNB  |  Cardiology: DM/DNB/MS  |  Gynaecology: DGO/MS/MD/DNB  |  Dentistry: BDS/MDS  |  Neurology: MD/MS/DNB  |  Nephrology: DM/DNB  |  Urology: MCh/DNB  |  Orthopaedics: MS/DNB  |  Paediatrics: DCH/DNB/MD  |  Oncology: DM/DNB/MCh/PhD  |  ENT: MS  |  Dermatology: MD  |  Psychiatry: MD  |  General Surgery: MS/DNB
